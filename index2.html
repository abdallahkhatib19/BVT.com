Who We Are

BVT is a group of passionate student volunteers dedicated to organizing impactful events in the fields of business and technology.Our team consists of 40 members from various disciplines, including design, marketing, and event coordination, all working together with a shared vision of success. Our team strives for innovation, offering unique solutions to meet the needs of our academic and professional community. Through hard work and commitment, we aim to make a difference in everything we do, proudly contributing to diverse areas such as design, programming, photography, and event management.
