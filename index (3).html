About Us

We are students from the University of Jordan, representing the School of Business since 2021. Our mission is to empower students by organizing impactful conferences and workshops. Through our bootcamps, we help students develop essential soft skills, preparing them to excel in the job market. By actively participating in conferences and events, we create opportunities for growth, learning, and networking, ensuring our members are well-equipped for future challenges.

Mission

Volunteerism and Awareness:
We aim to inspire students to actively participate in volunteer activities while raising awareness about social issues. Through campaigns, events, and conferences, we promote a culture of giving back and collective action to address societal challenges.

Partnerships and Resources:
Our mission includes collaborating with local businesses, organizations, and government entities to strengthen resources and support for impactful community projects and initiatives.

Students for the Future
We provide diverse opportunities for students to develop their personal and professional skills through bootcamps, workshops, and volunteering programs, ensuring they are well-prepared to succeed as future leaders in the job market.

Goals

Skill Development
To equip students with essential personal and professional skills through training programs, workshops, and hands-on volunteering experiences.

Impactful Community Projects
To initiate and support sustainable community projects that address pressing social issues and make a positive impact on society.

Collaboration
To build strong networks and partnerships with businesses, organizations, and institutions to expand opportunities and resources for our initiatives.
